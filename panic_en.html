How does it work?

What times are displayed ?
By default, the application shows times in the coming two hours. In real time mode, only times in the next hour are displayed.
See the previous or next schedule
You can double-tap on the left (or right) hand side to see the previous (next) schedule.
See the schedule at a different date
You can use the clock button to select a different date.
See your time of arrival
On the grid, by taping on a trip date, you will be shown the arrival times for the remainder of the trip.
Real time schedule
On the grid, you can use the central button to toggle the real time information. (Requires a working internet connection).

But, really ?

What does the arrow means on the favorites ?
The arrow represents the global direction (west, south...) of the indicated trip.
Different between arrival and departure
In some cases, there can be a difference between the arrival of the bus at the stop and its departure. You can choose what time to display globally via the preferences.
On the main grid, a long tap allows to switch between the different possibilities.
Order for the listing of stops
Well, yeah, only an alphabetical order is available, as displaying them in the order of the trips is a bit more tricky (for example, some lines may have different trips for some reasons).
Real time schedule in italic
Those times are only planned and may not be accurate
The schedule is outdated
Are you really using the latest version of the App ? (Note that there is no "online" update mechanism).
If the official schedule has been updated, it may be the author of this App is lazy or Apple may be nit-picking. Check the support website or contact the author.
When in doubt, check the paper timetables, the online website (updated in time usually) or the official STAR website.
